# How to use the TechCrunch News App

### In this article, we'll explain how to use the TechCrunch News app on OptiSigns.

* [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)
* [Creating a TechCrunch News Asset](#Creating)

The OptiSigns TechCrunch app allows you to display TechCrunch News on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Startups, AI, Venture, Security, Crypto, Apps, Fintech, Hardware news and more.

QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. TechCrunch News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)
