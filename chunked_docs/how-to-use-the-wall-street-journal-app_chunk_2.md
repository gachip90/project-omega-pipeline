# How to use the Wall Street Journal App

### In this article, we'll explain how to use The Wall Street Journal app on OptiSigns.

* [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)
* [Creating a The Wall Street Journal Asset](#Creating)

The OptiSigns CBC app allows you to display The Wall Street Journal on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Opinion pieces, World News stories, US Business pieces, Market news, Technology, and Lifestyle pieces.

QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. The Wall Street Journal can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)
