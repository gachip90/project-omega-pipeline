# How to Use the Simple Clock App

## Deploying a Simple Clock App

You can deploy your new Simple Clock app as an individual asset, or as part of a [Split Screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

To get your new Simple Clock asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410410870547)

This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414232467)

Here, select **Asset** under Content type, then hit **Change** next to Selected Asset.

Then, select your created Simple Clock Asset:

![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414234003)

Now hit **Save**. Your Simple Clock asset will now display on screen.

You can also deploy it as part of a split screen, allowing you to show other assets at the same time. See how in our [Split Screen app article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App) It can also be displayed in a Playlist or Schedule.
