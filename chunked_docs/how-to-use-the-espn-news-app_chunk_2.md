# How to use the ESPN News App

### In this article, we'll explain how to use the ESPN News app on OptiSigns.

* [What You'll Need](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041476213#WhatYouNeed)
* [Creating a ESPN News Asset](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041476213#Creating)

The OptiSigns ESPN app allows you to display ESPN News on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Top Headlines, NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, College Sports, and more.

QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. ESPN News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)
