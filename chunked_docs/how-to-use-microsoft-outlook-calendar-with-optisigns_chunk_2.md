# How to Use Microsoft Outlook Calendar with OptiSigns

## What You'll Need

* An OptiSigns account - [**Standard Plan** or higher](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)
* An [OptiSigns-enabled device](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021855653-What-hardware-and-devices-are-supported)
* A screen, [set up and paired with OptiSigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/18823504383891-OptiSigns-Getting-Started-Guide)

You may also need Administrator access on your Microsoft account. See our [Frequently Asked Questions](#FAQs) section for more on that.

Also, see our other article on [Setting Up an Outlook Calendar with Shared Permissions](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/45619214182803-How-to-Set-Up-an-Outlook-Calendar-App-with-Shared-Permissions), if that is what you need.
